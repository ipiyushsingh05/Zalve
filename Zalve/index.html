About the brand

Built small, made to feel big.

Zalve started as an idea in between classes — to build a lean streetwear label with clean lines, neutral tones and fits that feel good all day.

We work in small drops so we can obsess over details: fabric weight, stitching, how a hoodie sits on your shoulders, how cargos stack on your sneakers.

This first drop is limited. No fake hype, no fake scarcity — just pieces we actually wear ourselves.

Fabric first

Mid-weight cotton, soft fleece and durable blends that last more than just a season.

Made for movement

Fits tested in real life: college, commutes, café tables, late-night walks.
